Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Kegel Health is designed to respect your privacy. This policy explains what information is handled when you use our website, open-source codebase, Windows desktop app, and Android mobile app, and how we protect it.

1. Privacy by design

The web assessment does not require an account, email address, name, phone number, or payment information. We do not ask you to identify yourself to complete the questionnaire.

2. Website data

When you use the hosted website:

  • Questionnaire answers are processed to produce scores, triage pathways, and optional exercise plans. During an active session, answers may be stored in a temporary server-side session so you can continue the assessment and view results.
  • Session cookies are used only to maintain quiz progress and results for your current visit. They are not used for advertising profiles.
  • Standard server logs (such as IP address, browser type, and request time) may be recorded by the hosting provider for security and reliability. We do not use these logs to build marketing profiles.

3. Optional AI plan personalization

If cloud AI (for example, Google Gemini) is enabled on a deployment, anonymized assessment details needed to personalize a Kegel plan may be sent to that provider for processing. No name, email, or account identifier is included. If the service is unavailable or rate-limited, a template-based plan is used instead.

4. Desktop and mobile apps

The Windows and Android applications are intended to run primarily on your device. Assessment data in the apps is stored locally unless you choose a feature that explicitly transmits data (for example, an optional sync or update check). Because the apps are open source, you can review how data is handled in the published source code.

5. What we do not do

  • We do not sell your personal information.
  • We do not use the assessment for targeted advertising.
  • We do not require registration to use the core screening flow on the website.

6. Data retention

Website session data is temporary and tied to your browser session. It is not kept as a permanent user profile on our servers. When you close the browser or the session expires, that data is discarded. Local app data remains on your device until you uninstall the app or clear app storage.

7. Open source transparency

This project is open source. Security researchers, contributors, and users can inspect the code to verify how data flows through the website and applications. We encourage responsible disclosure of privacy or security concerns via the repository.

8. Children

This service is intended for adults. It is not directed at children under 18, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

9. International users

If you access the hosted website from outside the country where it is operated, your information may be processed where our hosting and optional AI providers operate, subject to their infrastructure and applicable law.

10. Changes to this policy

We may update this Privacy Policy as the project evolves. The “Last updated” date at the top of this page will change when we do. Continued use after an update means you accept the revised policy.

11. Contact

Privacy questions may be submitted through the project’s open-source repository issue tracker or other contact method listed there.
