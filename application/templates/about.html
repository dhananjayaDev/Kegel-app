This assessment implements the screening logic, scoring, and triage pathways described in our research documentation below.

Research methodology

The clinical foundation of Kegel Health was developed with support from Google Deep Research, which was used to synthesize and structure peer-reviewed literature on male erectile dysfunction, pelvic floor muscle training (PFMT), and related clinical triage pathways. Deep Research helped organize evidence, draft the underlying review, and align screening questions with published frameworks.

That material was reviewed, edited, and implemented as the automated scoring rules, pathways, and plan templates in this product. The full documentation—including references—is published below. Research-assisted synthesis does not replace independent clinical judgment or professional medical care.
